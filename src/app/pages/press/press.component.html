Press Kit

Fact Sheet

Developer: Synersteel Studio
Release Date: Nov 1 2024
Platforms: Steam
Website: valleyofshadow.com

Description

A narrative puzzle game that tells a poignant true story of loss and grief through the discovery of real-life home videos and photographs. Harness mysterious abilities to solve puzzles and heal a broken family.

Features

  • Experience the retelling of a traumatic event in the lives of the developers through the eyes of one of them (Anthony) as he attempts to heal.
  • Solve puzzles based around the most iconic magical spells in the fantasy genre: Light, Force Missile, & Fireball.
  • Discover nostalgic mementos from the 80's, 90's and 2000's, such as VHS tapes, floppy disks, and old-school game cartridges.
  • Each of the 12 old-school game parody cartridges has an accompanying puzzle inspired by the game itself.
  • Truly glimpse into the developers' lives by finding real home videos, family photos, and a digital recreation of their childhood home.

Quote

"When our father died I lost myself in video games. I was looking for solace, for peace, for an end to the pain of that loss. When I didn't find it there, I knew that we needed to create what I needed. Ten years later it is here, and we hope others can find what they need when it is their time too."

-Anthony Vaccaro, Developer
