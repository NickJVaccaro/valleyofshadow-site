Fact Sheet

Developer: Synersteel Studio
Release Date: 2023
Platforms: Steam
Website: valleyofshadow.com

Description

An autobiographical puzzle game about mortality. Cast Spells to unlock rooms and shed light on the darkness of a broken past. Relive a true journey of healing and explore a dark world to bring your family home.

Features

A True Story

Experience the dramatic retelling of a traumatic event in the lives of the developers, two brothers, through the eyes of one as he attempts to heal. REAL VHS tapes, photographs and answering machine audio piece together the fall and redemption of a once broken family.

Nostalgic Mementos

The Valley of Shadow is both a treasure and a tomb of nostalgia from the 80's, 90's and the 2000's. Each piece is significant, and each one familiar to the player. Some of the most popular pieces even have their own puzzles designed for them.

Fantasy Spells

Cast Light to not only find your way around dark areas, but also solve mind-bending puzzles revolving around Light & Shadow. Strategically manipulate your environment to control the shadows indirectly.
